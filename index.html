Our Craft

Raw steel.
Shaped by hand.

Every ForgedFloral piece begins as raw steel stock, worked by hand into shape — petals individually formed, curved, and textured before being welded onto a hand-forged stem.

No two pieces are identical. The finished steel is wire-brushed and sealed with a clear coat that preserves the raw, industrial character of the metal — warm, honest, and built to last.
